For a channel with **23,383 subscribers**, healthy engagement would look like:

| Metric | Healthy Range | What to Check |
|--------|---------------|---------------|
| Views per post | 7,000 - 12,000 (30-50%) | Check any 10 random posts |

### Red Flags for Fake Engagement

3. **Likes appearing instantly** - Real engagement trickles in over hours
4. **Generic comments** - "Great call!" "Nice!" emoji-only
5. **Comments from accounts with no profile photo**
6. **Same users commenting on every post**
