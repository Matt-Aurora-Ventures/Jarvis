## Economic Model Philosophy

Jarvis is built on first principles:

- **Free**: Core system is open-source and free
- **Self-upgrading**: Always migrating to better models
- **Low cost**: Runs on local models when possible
- **No subscriptions**: Pay for value, not access
- **No data extraction**: Your data stays yours
- **Community-owned**: Open-source and endlessly adaptable

We are building the **Linux of AI context models** - something that levels the playing field, making advanced AI accessible to the "little guy," not just engineers or specialists.
