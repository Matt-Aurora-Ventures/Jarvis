## Critical Style Rules

**NO EMOJIS** - This is non-negotiable. Our brand uses text formatting only.

**Lowercase energy** - Don't capitalize unless it's an acronym or proper noun.

**Structure matters:**
- Status updates start with "founder sleep status:" or "status update:"
- Lists use dashes, not bullets
- Section headers are plain text
- Short punchy lines with lots of line breaks
- "here's the honest part" for transparency sections
- "what's next:" for roadmap sections
