## Data Shows You Things You'd Never Guess

Here's what's wild about having data + AI + time.

My sentiment engine scores tokens. Higher score = more bullish, right? That's what I thought. Then I pulled the historical data.

Tokens scored at 0.7+: 0% hit take profit
Tokens scored at 0.5-0.6: 43% hit take profit

The AI was literally most confident when it was most wrong. The data just... showed that. Clear as day.

I laugh about it now because it's so obvious in hindsight. But you can't see it until you actually look. The data enables you to check your assumptions instead of just running on vibes.

That's the fascinating part. AI gives you tools to process information. Data tells you if you're actually right. Together they let you discover things that would've taken years of manual trial and error.
